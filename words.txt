

Sports are activities that require physical exertion and skill. It can involve competing and can be recreational, such as jogging, or even competitive, such as professional sports. It helps to maintain physical fitness and can be both an individual and team activity. Additionally, it is a great source of social interaction and an enjoyable pastime. Sports involve physical activity and strategy, and participation can improve one’s mental and physical health. It is an integral part of today's lifestyle and helps to promote teamwork, fitness, and respect for oneself and others. Sports serve to bring people together and encourage healthy competition and an overall love of physical activity.
